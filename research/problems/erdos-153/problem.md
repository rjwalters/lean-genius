Let $A$ be a finite Sidon set and $A+A=\{s_1<\cdots<s_t\}$. Is it true that\[\frac{1}{t}\sum_{1\leq i<t}(s_{i+1}-s_i)^2 \to \infty\]as $\lvert A\rvert\to \infty$?



A similar problem can be asked for infinite Sidon sets.
