# Problem: Erdős #200

## Statement

### Plain Language
Does the longest arithmetic progression of primes in $\{1,\ldots,N\}$ have length $o(\log N)$?



It follows from the prime number theorem that such a progression has length $\leq(1+o(1))\log N$.
