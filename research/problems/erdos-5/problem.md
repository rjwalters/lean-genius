# Problem: Erdős #5

## Statement

### Plain Language
Let $C\geq 0$. Is there an infinite sequence of $n_i$ such that\[\lim_{i\to \infty}\frac{p_{n_i+1}-p_{n_i}}{\log n_i}=C?\]
